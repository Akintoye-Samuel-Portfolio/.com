About Me

Senior designer, systems thinker, video & motion creator.

I'm a Senior Graphic Designer with over seven years of experience helping businesses build strong visual identities through branding, UI/UX design, motion graphics, video editing and digital marketing.

I design with intent — every layout, colour and interaction is built to improve brand visibility, engage audiences, increase conversions and communicate ideas clearly. I also produce AI-generated talking videos for businesses, brands, education and marketing using professional AI video tools.
